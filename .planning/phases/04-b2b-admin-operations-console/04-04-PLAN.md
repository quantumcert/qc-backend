<threat_model>

## Trust Boundaries

- Boundary A: payment provider webhook -> backend credit ledger.
- Boundary B: application credits -> financial wallet/deposits.
- Boundary C: admin credit adjustment -> tenant balance projection.

## Threats

- **HIGH: Webhook forjado ou replay duplica creditos.** Mitigation:
  `validateWebhook`, dedupe por provider/eventId/hash, testes de replay e
  assinatura invalida.
- **HIGH: Credito deriva de wallet custodial/financial balance.** Mitigation:
  `CreditLedgerEntry` e fonte de verdade; `UserWallet` nao e dependencia do
  ledger.
- **HIGH: Admin ajusta creditos sem motivo/audit.** Mitigation:
  reason obrigatorio e `AdminAuditLog` na mesma transacao.
- **MEDIUM: Reserva falha e consome duas vezes.** Mitigation:
  idempotency key/reference object em reserve/consume/release.
- **MEDIUM: Acoplamento prematuro a Transfero.** Mitigation:
  provider interface + fake provider; Transfero marcado como candidato TBD.
</threat_model>
